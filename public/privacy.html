PRIVACY POLICY

Your privacy and data security matter to us

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

1. About Quantumchem

Quantumchem is a comprehensive digital platform offering:

  • Engineering Chemistry courses
  • Lecture notes and tutorials
  • Laboratory experiments and safety protocols
  • Chemical databases (acids, bases, salts, organic compounds, indicators, solvents, gases)
  • Secure user authentication and personalized access

The platform is accessible through navigation sections including Home, Chemicals, Tutorials, Lectures, Features, Resources, Blog, Documentation, and Help Center.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & Login Information

When you use features such as "Get Started", "Explore Main Platform", or Secure Access Flow, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Login identifiers (Google OAuth or manual login)
  • Temporary access credentials
  • Authentication tokens
Security Note: All authentication data is securely stored in MongoDB with industry-standard encryption.

2.2 Platform Usage Data

When you browse content, we may collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Pages viewed
  • Time spent on platform
  • Device and browser type
  • IP address (anonymized)

Important: This data cannot personally identify you.

3. Secure Access Flow

Quantumchem uses an enterprise-grade authentication system, which includes:

  • Sign-up with email verification
  • Temporary password (valid for 4 days)
  • Secure token-based login
  • Auto-expiry of credentials
  • Password reset with verification

This system is designed to protect user accounts and prevent unauthorized access.

4. Cookies & Session Management

Cookies and local storage are used to:

  • Maintain secure login sessions
  • Remember user preferences
  • Protect authenticated pages
  • Improve user experience
Privacy Protection: No sensitive data (passwords or personal details) is stored in cookies.

5. Google Login (OAuth)

If you choose Google Sign-In:

  • We access only your name and email
  • No access to contacts, files, or private Google data
  • Authentication is handled securely by Google

This complies with Google API Services User Data Policy.

6. Educational Content Disclaimer

Content such as water technology, fuels and combustion, corrosion control, polymer chemistry, and laboratory experiments is provided strictly for educational purposes.

Disclaimer: Quantumchem does not guarantee laboratory outcomes and is not responsible for misuse of information. Always follow official MSDS and laboratory safety standards.

7. Chemical Database Disclaimer

Our chemical database includes acids, bases, salts, organic compounds, indicators, solvents, and gases.

All data is for academic reference only. Always follow official MSDS and laboratory safety standards.
