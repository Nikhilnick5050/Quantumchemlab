Project Overview

QuantumChem is an independent academic project developed by students of Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering (PCCOE). Our goal is to create a simple, educational platform that helps students understand chemical information clearly.

Project Purpose

QuantumChem provides a digital platform for learning about chemical properties, safety information, and basic chemical concepts in a student-friendly way.

Independent Academic Project

QuantumChem is developed independently by students. It does not represent official PCCOE laboratory protocols, procedures, or institutional guidelines.

Project Objectives

Educational Goals

  • Support undergraduate chemistry learning
  • Provide clear chemical information for students
  • Help understand basic chemical concepts
  • Make chemical information accessible and simple

Important Note

QuantumChem is for educational purposes only. It does not replace professional laboratory training, official MSDS documents, or institutional safety protocols.

Data Sources

QuantumChem uses publicly available chemical information from reputable sources:

Source Purpose
PubChem Database Chemical structure and property information
ChemSpider Database Data verification and cross-reference
Public Safety Resources General safety information

Data Accuracy

We strive to provide accurate information but make no guarantees about completeness or absolute accuracy. Always verify critical information from official sources.

Safety Awareness

QuantumChem provides basic safety information for educational awareness. For actual laboratory work:

  • Always use official MSDS sheets
  • Follow institutional safety protocols
  • Use proper protective equipment
  • Work under qualified supervision

Responsible Use

You are responsible for how you use information from QuantumChem. Use caution, common sense, and always prioritize safety.
