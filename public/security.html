How We Protect Your Account

QuantumChem offers two secure authentication methods designed for your convenience and safety

Primary Method

Email Verification Login

1
Request Login

Enter your verified email address on the login page

2
Receive Temporary Password

A secure temporary password is sent to your email instantly

3
Secure Access

Use the temporary password to log in securely

4
Auto-Expiry Protection

Temporary passwords automatically expire after 4 days for security

Security Feature: Passwords expire automatically after 4 days. You can request a new password reset at any time through the secure reset flow.

Alternative Method

Google OAuth 2.0

1
Click Google Sign-In

Select "Continue with Google" on the login page

2
Authorize via Google

Authenticate through Google's secure OAuth 2.0 flow

3
Instant Access

Gain immediate secure access to your QuantumChem account

Trusted Authentication: Google OAuth 2.0 is an industry-standard protocol trusted by millions of applications worldwide.

Password Reset Flow

1
Initiate Reset

Click "Forgot Password" or "Reset Password" on the login page

2
Secure Email Delivery

Receive a new temporary password via secure email

3
Regain Access

Log in with your new temporary password immediately

Always Available: Password reset is available 24/7 with instant email delivery. Old passwords are invalidated immediately upon reset.

User Responsibilities

Security is a shared responsibility. Help us protect your account by:

  • Using strong, unique passwords for your account
  • Keeping your login credentials private and secure
  • Logging out from shared or public devices
  • Being cautious of phishing attempts and suspicious emails
  • Keeping your browser and operating system updated
