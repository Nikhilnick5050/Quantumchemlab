SAFETY POLICY

Your safety is our priority - Please read carefully before using our platform

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to QuantumChem ("we", "our", or "us").

QuantumChem is an advanced engineering chemistry learning and research platform offering:

  • Chemical databases
  • Engineering chemistry course materials
  • Laboratory tutorials and manuals
  • Safety guidelines and protocols
  • AI-assisted chemical analysis
  • Interactive molecular visualization

This Safety Policy explains the safe use, limitations, responsibilities, and risks associated with using the QuantumChem platform.

2. Scope of This Safety Policy

This Safety Policy applies to all sections of QuantumChem, including but not limited to:

  • Home
  • Chemicals
  • Tutorials
  • Lectures
  • Features
  • Blog
  • Documentation
  • Safety
  • Account & Login pages

It applies to all users, including visitors, registered users, students, researchers, and educators.

3. Educational-Only Platform Disclaimer

Important: QuantumChem is a learning and reference platform only.

We do NOT:

  • Conduct physical experiments
  • Supervise laboratories
  • Provide professional or industrial instructions

All information is intended strictly for academic and educational purposes.

4. User Account & Access Safety

QuantumChem provides secure access through:

  • Manual login
  • Google Sign-In (OAuth)

Users are responsible for:

  • Maintaining account confidentiality
  • Logging out from shared devices
  • Using platform resources responsibly

QuantumChem is not liable for misuse of accounts or credentials.

5. Chemical Database Safety Notice

QuantumChem hosts a database of 1000+ laboratory chemicals, including:

  • Acids
  • Bases
  • Salts
  • Organic compounds
  • Indicators
  • Solvents
  • Gases

Each chemical entry may include:

  • Molecular formula and weight
  • Classification (strong/weak, acidic/basic)
  • Safety notes
  • MSDS references
  • Visual molecular data
Critical Safety Note: Chemical data is informational only and must never replace official SDS/MSDS documents.

6. AI Analysis & Predictive Features Safety

QuantumChem may provide AI-based features such as:

  • Property prediction
  • Reaction analysis
  • Hazard estimation
  • Safety recommendations
AI Limitations: These features:
  • Are generated algorithmically
  • May not be 100% accurate
  • Must not be used for real-world decision-making

AI outputs are guidance tools, not authoritative instructions.

7. 3D Molecular Visualization Safety

Interactive 3D molecular structures are provided for:

  • Learning molecular geometry
  • Understanding bonding and orbitals
  • Visual education

These visualizations are representational models and must not be used as experimental substitutes.

9. Laboratory Safety Fundamentals

Users performing any experiment must:

  • Follow institutional laboratory rules
  • Wear appropriate PPE (lab coat, goggles, gloves)
  • Avoid eating or drinking in labs
  • Handle chemicals with care
  • Read procedures fully before starting
Disclaimer: QuantumChem assumes no control over real-world laboratory conditions.

10. Equipment & Instrument Safety

Laboratory instruments referenced on QuantumChem (pH meter, conductivity meter, spectrophotometer, electrolysis setup, etc.) must be:

  • Operated only by trained individuals
  • Inspected before use
  • Used according to manufacturer guidelines
Warning: Improper use may cause injury or damage.

11. Waste Disposal & Environmental Safety

Users must:

  • Follow laboratory waste disposal rules
  • Separate hazardous, liquid, and solid waste
  • Never dispose chemicals improperly

QuantumChem does not manage or supervise waste handling.

12. Course Content & Lecture Safety

Engineering Chemistry courses, including:

  • Water Technology & Green Chemistry
  • Fuels and Combustion
  • Corrosion and Corrosion Control
  • Polymers and Nanomaterials

are theoretical and academic in nature.

Numerical problems, diagrams, and explanations are provided for learning purposes only.

13. Virtual Labs, PDFs & Videos

QuantumChem may provide:

  • Downloadable lab manuals (PDF)

  • Virtual lab simulations

These resources:

  • Do not replace physical lab training
  • Do not guarantee safety compliance
  • Are supplementary learning tools

14. Safety Monitoring Disclaimer

Any references to "24/7 Safety Monitoring" refer to:

15. Prohibited Use

Users must NOT:

  • Perform experiments without supervision
  • Use chemical data for illegal activities
  • Rely solely on QuantumChem for safety decisions
  • Attempt unsafe or unauthorized experiments
Violation may result in account restriction.

QuantumChem shall not be held liable for:

  • Incorrect experimental outcomes
  • Misuse of information

Use of the platform is entirely at the user's own risk.
